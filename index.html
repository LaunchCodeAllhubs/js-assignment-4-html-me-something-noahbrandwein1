The second game... Super Smash Bros. Brawl!

The next game I played debuted on the Nintendo Wii. It added characters like: Toon Link for The Legend of Zelda, Pokémon Trainer (who controls Squirtle, Ivysaur, and Charizard) and Lucario for Pokémon, and Ike for Fire Emblem.
